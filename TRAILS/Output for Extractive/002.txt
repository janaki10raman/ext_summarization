China net cafe culture crackdown

Chinese authorities closed 12,575 net cafes in the closing months of 2004, the country's government said.The official Xinhua News Agency said the crackdown was carried out to create a "safer environment for young people in China".China also tries to dictate the types of computer games people can play to limit the amount of violence people are exposed to.Laws on net cafe opening hours and who can use them were introduced in 2002 following a fire at one cafe that killed 25 people.In August 2004 Chinese authorities shut down 700 websites and arrested 224 people in a crackdown on net porn.
